How to roll 20 on D20

Introduction

This technical documentation was made in learning and improving purposes.

It is settled in RPG (role play gaming) environment, where in some popular games rolling 20 on D20 is a critical success - the thing most desired by players and GMs (Game Masters).

Below You will get some advises how to make your rolls more effective.

What you need to know

D20 is a kind of dice, which has 20 walls. On each wall is set a number in range 1-20.

Walls are set equally which means you have statisticly only 5% chance of rolling each number on D20.

There are many types of D20. We can divide type in view of:

  • size
  • color
  • weight

Container to thrown

There are plenty of ways throwing down the dices.

If you want increase your chances you need to improve every aspect, even the surface you thrown dice to.

There are few examples of surfaces:

  • ground
  • table
  • glass
  • box
  • container

Container is the best way to improve your chances.

Inside the smal container you can use special materil, which makes your dice roll better and with connection with proper hand shaking you will successful in rolling 20 on D20 more than 5%.

Hand shaking process

This section is decribing the most effective way of improving your chances - shaking. When you pick up your dice to roll to your hand you need to focus on few aspects. First of it is your hand opening level. You can have your hand:
  • totally open
  • slightly closed - little boat shape
  • finger closed
  • almost closed - leave little space for roling the dice in your hand
  • totally closed without any possibility to change it orientation in your hand
Second is the hand orientation. You can set your hand:
  • vertically
  • horizontally
  • in 30 degree
  • in 45 degree
  • in 60 degree
Last very important thing is the direction of shaking the hand. Below you can meet few of possibilities:
  • up and down
  • right and left
  • rotation in eclipse shape
  • rotation the hand in wrist axis
  • shaking lineary in range 30-60 degree

Making reality impossible

I don't recommend it, but if you want to have your "lucky dice" you should follow below:
  • take some grinding tool
  • pick up your lucky dice
  • find the opposite wall to your 20 (in 99.99% of cases it is number 1)
  • grind slightly edges around the numer to have lower friction factor
  • clean up the edges and enjoy with cheating
